About Kodrael Cuts

Founded with a passion for precision and style, Kodrael Cuts has been redefining the barbershop experience since 2015.

At Kodrael Cuts, we believe that a great haircut is more than just a service—it's an experience. Our skilled barbers combine traditional techniques with modern trends to create styles that not only look great but also reflect your personality.

From classic cuts to contemporary styles, we offer a comprehensive range of grooming services tailored to meet your unique needs. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the chair, creating an atmosphere where you can relax, recharge, and leave feeling your absolute best.

Meet Our CEO - Israel Oko

Israel Oko is the visionary behind Kodrael Cuts, bringing over 15 years of experience in the grooming industry. With a background in both traditional barbering and modern styling techniques, Israel has cultivated a team of talented professionals who share his commitment to excellence.

"Every haircut tells a story," Israel says. "Our job is to help our clients tell their best story through style. We're not just cutting hair; we're crafting confidence."

Under Israel's leadership, Kodrael Cuts has grown from a single-chair operation to a premier destination for quality grooming services, earning recognition for both technical skill and exceptional customer service.
